This is the resolution of the Goodylabs' Internship Exercise made by the candidate Gabriel Moreira.

Bachelor Degree in Computer Science

At the second half of 2013 I got my Bachelor Degree in Computer Science at UFPE (Federal University of Pernambuco). My final project was about semantic web and open data: I developed a tool to convert government financial data available on SOAP web-services into RDF to publish on the web.

E-Gov Award 2013 in Public Services

In 2013, working for the company Facilit Tecnologia, I was part of the development team of the LAI Portal. This project won the E-Gov Award 2013 in the category Public Services. Itwas made to satisfy the, at the moment new, Law of Access to Information. Using this web portal all departments of the government of Pernambuco State could insert their information and this would be stored in structured format and would be well displayed for the citizens.

Best Alfresco's Systems Integrator Partner of 2012

When was an intern at the company Zaizi in Seville, Spain, we were chosen the by Alfresco, the owner of the main platform we worked with, the best system integrator partner of 2012.
